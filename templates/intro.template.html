Welcome to Prompter

Prompter is a lightweight web app that generates creative prompts for AI models entirely in your browser.

It offers twelve prompt categories and six interface languages with more than 53 million combinations per language.

Use the copy and share buttons to reuse or publish prompts. Select the star icon to save favorites locally or online when signed in.

Join the community WhatsApp group for updates and new ideas.

Our vision is to spark creativity by making prompt generation quick, fun and accessible.

You can support Prompter by subscribing on the Pro page or by clicking the support button.
